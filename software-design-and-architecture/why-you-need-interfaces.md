# Why You Need Interfaces

<!-- tl;dr starts -->

Interfaces are needed for Abstraction -> Inheritance -> Polymorphism, Contracts, Loose Coupling, Cross-cutting Concerns, Generics, Marker, DI, Modularity, Scaling, Test case (Reality).
